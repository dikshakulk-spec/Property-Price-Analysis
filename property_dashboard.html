Location & Demand > Everything Else
Location, affordability, and demand drive property investments far more than size or amenities. Proximity to schools and malls matters most.
🏢
Apartments Dominate the Market
Apartments lead in sales/rentals due to affordability, prime urban locations, modern amenities, and lower maintenance — best investment choice.
🏫
Infrastructure Drives Value
Proximity to schools, malls, and hospitals increases property value. Location 25 & 34 show this clearly with highest combined access scores.

Location 32 Best Strategy
Best returns: pre-1920 or post-2000 builds, renovated 2000s–2020s, near 1 school or mall. Avoid: paying extra for pools, garages, or extra schools.

East Region Growth Potential
Low property count due to weak demand, NOT lack of infrastructure. With future development, East could offer affordable entry points for investors.

Business Strategy
Developers should prioritize strategic location selection, infrastructure improvement near schools & malls, and focus on apartment developments.
